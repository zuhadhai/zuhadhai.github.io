Zuhad Hai

Welcome! I am a Ph.D. candidate in Political Science at Stanford University. I am interested in International Relations and Political Economy.

I study the impact of scientific and technological change on politics. A common theme across my research is how communities react to the creation of new knowledge, whether in the laboratory or the factory floor. Since scientific and technological innovations often do not respect national borders, most of my work looks at these questions through an international lens.
